Jeopardy Game Instructions

Objective:

Answer questions correctly to earn points. The team with the most points at the end wins.

Setting Up:

Teams: Form your teams and enter each teamâ€™s name.

Start the Game: Create your teams in caplocks, once you have your desired teams click "START" to begin!

How to Play:

Selecting Questions: Teams take turns choosing a category and a point value. Click on the chosen question to reveal it.

Answering Questions: The team that picked the question answers first. Correct answers earn the points. Incorrect answers lock the questions turning them dark blue, which stop anyone else from answering them.

Winning the Game: The team with the most points after Final Jeopardy wins. Have fun and good luck!
